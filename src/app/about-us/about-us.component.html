Methods for Recruiting and Retaining Donors
Warm-glow is an important factor to stress when recruiting new donors and working to turn them into repeat donors. According to de Kort and Merz, trying to reignite feelings of warm-glow can be a useful intervention, especially for those with lower initial levels of warm-glow
“Furthermore, getting donors past their fourth donation is the point at which they start to consider being a blood donor as part of their identity,” Merz said. “At this point, they are more likely to become life-long donors.”
